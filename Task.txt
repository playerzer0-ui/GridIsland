# Grid Island

this is a island survival based game

you just need to move along the grid and dodge all potential enemy attacks

see how long you survive

# enemies

the enemies would be:

- a ball shooting at you
- a wave coming in at vertical

# scoring

a score will be displayed at top left, one hit on player and game over

(380, 240) topLeft
(380, 420) bottomLeft
(810, 240) topRight
(810, 420) bottomRight

60px jumpX

# wave

a timer of 4 seconds countdown, while that countdown, a warning will appear

once it ends, wave come in

and go back
